MERCEDES-BENZ

Mercedes-Benz is one of the companies we have been collaborating with for more than 20 years, preparing the latest and most advanced technologies, thus creating their car in a space shuttle. Also, in recent years, we provide maintenance in terms of diagnostic systems in the car, for a better structured and legible diagnosis.

Since 2001, we have created a friendly and fraternal relationship with the Mercedes-Benz team, by sponsoring the latest cars among the latest equipment. Since 2010, we have also integrated into the Formula 1 team, by equipping it with state-of-the-art audio systems and with real-time car location and diagnostic systems.
